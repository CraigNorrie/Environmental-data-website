Thorndyke Bay environmental data

Thorndyke Bay monitoring instruments are located within the Baywater Shellfish shellfish farm. Baywater shellfish is an intertidal shellfish farm. Environmental monitoring instruments are located on oyster lines that are anchored into the ground and are exposed every tidal cycle.

Site Location

Sensors at Thorndyke Bay are deployed on a commercial shellfish farm. The sensors are located in an intertidal area at a tidal height of approximately 0m above mean low tide.

Temperature data

Temperature data was collected from hobo DO and temp logger. Data was collected every 15 minutes and daily averages were calculated. Note this data included information from when individuals were out of the water.

Disolved oxygen data
