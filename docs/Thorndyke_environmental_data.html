Thorndyke Bay environmental data

Thorndyke Bay monitoring instruments are located within the Baywater Shellfish shellfish farm. Baywater shellfish is an intertidal shellfish farm. Environmental monitoring instruments are located on oyster lines that are anchored into the ground and are exposed every tidal cycle.
