Hood Head environmental data

Hood Head site is located within Blue Dot Sea Farm. Blue Dot Sea Farm is a subtidal shellfish and seaweed farm. Environmental monitoring instruments are deployed on hanging SEAPA oyster cages or suspended directly from backbone lines.

Temperature data

Temperature data was collected from hobo DO and temp logger. Data was collected every 15 minutes and daily averages were calculated.

The hobo logger was lost from this stite between August and October.

Disolved oxygen data

Dissolved oxygen data was collected using a hobo DO and temp logger. Data was collected every 15 minutes and daily averages were calculated. Note this data included information from when individuals were out of the water
